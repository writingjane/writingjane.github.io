About Me

In 2019, I started a movie blog and that's where my writing skills got fine-tuned coupled with the courses I currently take continuously to better my skills.
Prior to this, I've been creating contents for social media and still do till date.
I am interested in communicating meaningful experiences in product and service design for organizations. If that is you, please get in touch
